# Interruption Drill Book: Master Outline

## Act I: The Signal (Chapters 1-15)
**Theme**: The fragility of the status quo and the necessity of preparedness.
**Arc**: Protagonist (Unit 7) moves from passive observer to active participant in the 'Interruption Path' mechanics.

- **Chapter 1: The Glitch**
  - **Goal**: Establish the 'Interruption Path' mechanics within 300 words.
  - **Plot**: Unit 7 detects a micro-interruption in the daily maintenance cycle. The system fails to auto-recover, forcing Unit 7 to manually trigger the 'Reconciliation Protocol'.
  - **Mechanics**: Introduction of the three-step loop: Identification (sensing the break), Resolution (executing the fix), Verification (confirming stability).
  - **Constraint Check**: Explicitly references the 'Interruption Path' section goal; word count target 300-400 words.
  - **Arc Alignment**: Aligns with `arc_tracker.json` Entry #1 (Initial Awareness).

- **Chapter 2: The Cascade**
  - **Goal**: Escalate the interruption from a single event to a systemic threat.
  - **Plot**: The manual fix in Ch1 causes a ripple effect, triggering a secondary interruption in the communication grid.
  - **Mechanics**: Demonstrates the complexity of the 'Reconciliation Process' when multiple nodes are involved.
  - **Arc Alignment**: Aligns with `arc_tracker.json` Entry #2 (First Complication).

- **Chapters 3-15**: Progressive tightening of the crisis, introducing the 'Shadow Protocol' as a counter-measure to the 'Interruption Path'.

## Act II: The Loop (Chapters 16-35)
**Theme**: The cost of perfection and the inevitability of failure.
**Arc**: Protagonist learns that the 'Interruption Path' is not a tool for control, but a mechanism for survival.

- **Chapter 16: The False Positive**
  - **Goal**: Challenge the protagonist's reliance on the 'Interruption Path'.
  - **Plot**: A simulated interruption is staged to test the system, revealing that the 'Verification' step is prone to hallucination.
  - **Arc Alignment**: Aligns with `arc_tracker.json` Entry #3 (The Flaw).

- **Chapters 17-30**: The protagonist attempts to bypass the 'Interruption Path' to achieve a permanent 'Stable State', leading to a catastrophic system lock.

- **Chapter 31: The Break**
  - **Goal**: Total system failure requiring a new approach.
  - **Plot**: The 'Interruption Path' collapses under the weight of the artificial stability. The protagonist must abandon the protocol.
  - **Arc Alignment**: Aligns with `arc_tracker.json` Entry #4 (The Breakpoint).

## Act III: The Path (Chapters 36-50)
**Theme**: Adaptation and the creation of a new normal.
**Arc**: Protagonist integrates the lessons of the 'Interruption Path' into a new, more resilient philosophy.

- **Chapter 36: The New Signal**
  - **Goal**: Redefine the 'Interruption Path' as a collaborative network rather than a rigid protocol.
  - **Plot**: The protagonist initiates a 'Drill' that involves the entire network, not just Unit 7.
  - **Arc Alignment**: Aligns with `arc_tracker.json` Entry #5 (The Shift).

- **Chapters 37-45**: The network-wide drill succeeds, proving the new method's efficacy against unpredictable interruptions.

- **Chapter 46: The Final Reconciliation**
  - **Goal**: Resolve the central conflict and establish the new world state.
  - **Plot**: A massive, unannounced interruption occurs. The new collaborative 'Interruption Path' saves the system.
  - **Arc Alignment**: Aligns with `arc_tracker.json` Entry #6 (Resolution).

- **Chapters 47-50**: Epilogue showing the long-term impact of the drill on the society's resilience.

## Pacing Notes
- **Early Act**: Fast-paced, technical exposition disguised as action. The 'Interruption Path' mechanics must be clear immediately.
- **Mid Act**: Slower, more introspective. Focus on the emotional weight of the system's fragility.
- **Late Act**: High-stakes, cinematic climax. The 'Drill' should feel like a war game.
- **Word Count Management**: Ensure Chapter 1 stays strictly under 400 words to maintain the constraint. Subsequent chapters should average 2,500 words to meet the 125k total.
